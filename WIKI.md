# Wiki Registry

This repository contains two independent knowledge bases. Treat each as its own ownership
boundary: do not move or cross-list material between them merely because their subjects overlap.

| Wiki | Path | Scope | Articles | Index |
|------|------|-------|----------|-------|
| Great Work Utah | `wiki/` | High-impact work with a substantive Utah connection | See generated views | [index](wiki/views/index.md) |
| Shadow Wiki | `shadow_wiki/` | Exceptional work without a substantive Utah connection | 0 | [index](shadow_wiki/wiki/index.md) |

## Wiki Instance Details

### Great Work Utah

The public, LLM-first wiki. Its schema and workflow are defined in [wiki/WIKI.md](wiki/WIKI.md).

### Shadow Wiki

Created: 2026-07-16

The private-by-interface companion knowledge base for work that meets the same high-impact bar
but has no substantive Utah connection. It is tracked in Git, but excluded from the website,
the production build, and the public agent manual.

Initial subtopics are intentionally emergent. Create a topic directory when the first article
requires it; do not predeclare a taxonomy before the corpus exists.

Conventions and source preferences are defined in
[shadow_wiki/WIKI.md](shadow_wiki/WIKI.md). Prefer primary sources and verbatim captures.
