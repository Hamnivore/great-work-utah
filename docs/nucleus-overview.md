# The Nucleus Institute — Overview

> Source: <https://www.nucleusutah.org/>

## Mission

The Nucleus Institute brings together Utah's universities, industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and policymakers to turn big ideas into real-world solutions.

> **Innovation through collaboration.** Whether you're learning, building, investing, or leading—Nucleus is where the future takes shape.

Nucleus drives Utah's Innovation Economy through **five key pillars** that support ideas, talent, and progress.

## The Five Pillars

### 1. Talent Hub — Matching talent to opportunity

Connects students, researchers, founders, and mentors to the people and projects that move innovation forward. Through programs like Talent Hub, Nucleus helps Utah's workforce plug into high-impact work across sectors.

### 2. Commercialization — Turning research into real-world solutions

Helps university research and technologies move from concept to company. When an idea is ready for market, Nucleus helps innovators tap into the education, connections, and support to help it grow. Programs guide founders through the early stages of building—from business models to investor readiness.

### 3. Capital — Funding for early-stage innovation

Closes funding gaps that often block early ideas from scaling. Through programs including the **Nucleus Fund** and **Nucleus Grow**, Nucleus offers both equity and non-dilutive support for research-backed ventures.

### 4. Solutions — Building the systems that help innovation thrive

Strengthens Utah's startup ecosystem through long-term, high-impact initiatives. Supports projects that shape the environment for innovation—intentional policy development, strategic partnerships, and impactful student experiences. From enabling better regulation to launching programs that grow Utah's talent and reach, this initiative helps keep Utah a top place in the world to build.

### 5. Place — Where Utah's innovation finds space to grow

Nucleus is working toward a future where innovation districts across Utah form a statewide network—each one a spoke in a larger system designed to drive growth, collaboration, and discovery.

At the center is **Convergence Hall**, the flagship space at The Point. It's more than just a building—it's the physical embodiment of Nucleus: open, cross-sector, and built for what's next. Together, these stations will anchor innovation in communities across Utah, connecting local talent to shared opportunity.
