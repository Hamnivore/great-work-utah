## Why It Mattered

Zip gave consumer computing a removable high-capacity standard during the awkward gap between the floppy and the USB stick. For five years, the format mattered enormously to a generation of designers, students, and hobbyists. Then it stopped mattering at all.

## Lessons for Builders

Transitional standards are still standards. A product that ships at the right moment for five years can be more useful than a product engineered to last forever and shipped too early.
