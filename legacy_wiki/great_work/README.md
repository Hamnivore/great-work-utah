## The tier convention

- **S** — definitively world-changing; the work is in textbooks or the world is measurably different because it happened in Utah
- **A** — strong contribution; the case is clear even if the impact is bounded
- **B** — real contribution with caveats; mattered at a sector or regional level
- **C** — plausible but limited
- **D** — weak but in
- **F** — placeholder stub

Speculative tiers:

- **P-A** — plausible future A/S-tier work with strong technical or adoption signals; not yet historically proven
- **P-B** — promising frontier work with meaningful signal; needs validation, scale, or durable impact
- **P-C** — weak but interesting watchlist item
