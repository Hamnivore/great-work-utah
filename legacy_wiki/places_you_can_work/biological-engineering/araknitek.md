Spider silk proteins (stronger-per-weight than steel, more elastic than Kevlar, biocompatible) from the USU Randy Lewis lab — one of the world's leading synthetic spider silk programs. Target applications: high-performance fabrics, composite fibers, medical implant coatings, sutures, 3D-printed medical products. The science is real. The commercialization history is not: spider silk has been "5 years away" for 30+ years. Bolt Threads (CA) raised $200M+ and struggled. **Verify that Araknitek is still active before spending time on this entry.** If the Randy Lewis lab has a new spin-out or a different commercialization vehicle, that supersedes this entry.
