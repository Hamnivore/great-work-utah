# Batu Biologics

**Tier:** F — anti-angiogenic immuno-oncology, limited public information
**Domain:** Biological engineering — tumor vasculature targeting
**Type:** Company (private, U of U spinout)
**Stage:** Preclinical (unknown funding)
**Location:** Salt Lake City, UT
**Website:** Unknown

Targets the tumor vasculature (blood vessels feeding tumors) as an immuno-oncology strategy. "Achilles Heel of cancer" framing — cutting off the tumor's blood supply has been an oncology strategy since Judah Folkman's work in the 1970s; bevacizumab (Avastin) is already approved. What Batu is doing differently is not clearly public. Part of U of U TLO startup portfolio. Research further if you're specifically interested in anti-angiogenic approaches to solid tumor treatment.
