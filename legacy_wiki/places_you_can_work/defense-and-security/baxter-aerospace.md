# Baxter Aerospace

**Tier:** D — real DoD products, not transformative
**Domain:** Defense & security — UAS platforms and deployable communications
**Type:** Company (private)
**Stage:** Series A ($6M, DoD Accelerator / Accuidity Capital, 2025)
**Location:** St. George, UT
**Website:** [baxteraerospace.com](https://www.baxteraerospace.com)

Makes the Dragonfly UAS and Deployable Communications Kits for defense, intelligence, and emergency response. Founded after the 2017 Eagle Creek Fire; designed for austere, dynamic environments where connectivity and air surveillance are degraded. Products combine edge connectivity hardware with UAS integration. DoD validation through the Accuidity Capital accelerator ($6M Series A in 2025). Real products, real customers, but not transformative at the technology or mission level — an execution-mode defense supplier in a crowded field.
