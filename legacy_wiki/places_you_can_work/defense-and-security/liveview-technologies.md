# LiveView Technologies (LVT)

**Tier:** D — strong commercial traction, not world-changing
**Domain:** Defense & security — AI-driven mobile surveillance
**Type:** Company (private)
**Stage:** Growth ($135M raised; Sorenson Capital, Larry H. Miller Group investors)
**Location:** American Fork, UT
**Website:** [lvt.com](https://www.lvt.com)

AI-powered mobile surveillance units: solar-powered trailer-mounted cameras with AI analytics for construction sites, retail, events, and public safety. 6,000+ deployments. $135M raised from Sorenson Capital and the Larry H. Miller Group. Real commercial scale but not a hard technical problem — this is product execution in an established market (security cameras + AI analytics). The world-changing argument is weak: LVT is a competent execution company, not a mission-driven one. Included because it's one of Utah's largest private tech companies and represents the upper bound of what "not world-changing enough" looks like.
