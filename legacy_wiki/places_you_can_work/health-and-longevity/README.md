# Health & Longevity

Keeping humans alive and well longer. Includes aging biology, drug discovery, diagnostics, genomics, and medical devices. Distinct from [biological engineering](../biological-engineering/), which covers programming living systems as a platform.

**A note on Utah:** Utah has a surprisingly strong health research ecosystem, anchored by the University of Utah's Huntsman Cancer Institute and a unique research asset: the Utah Population Database (UPDB) — the world's largest multigenerational health/genealogy database, linking millions of people across generations to medical and public health records. The LDS community's genealogy work made this possible. BRCA1, BRCA2, and several other landmark disease genes were identified here. That research foundation has seeded real companies.

## Ecosystem context

- **Utah Population Database (UPDB)** — World's largest multigenerational genealogy + health records database. Research infrastructure at U of U. Not a company, but the reason Utah punches above its weight in genomics and population health research.
- **Altitude Lab** — Biotech accelerator in Salt Lake City, anchored by Recursion CEO Chris Gibson. Supports early-stage biotechs.
- **U2TAH** — $22.5M therapeutics accelerator launched at Huntsman Cancer Institute in 2024 to spin out more drug discovery companies.
