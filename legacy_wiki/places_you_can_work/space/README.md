# Space

Expanding humanity's presence beyond Earth — launch, in-space infrastructure, sensing, planetary defense, and eventually living off-world.

**A note on Utah:** Utah's space sector is dominated by defense primes and supply chain — Northrop Grumman's solid rocket motor facility in Promontory (SLS, Orion abort system), RAM Aviation's precision propulsion valves (SpaceX, ISS, Dream Chaser), and a deep aerospace manufacturing ecosystem organized through 47G. Frontier space startups (launch vehicles, in-space manufacturing, novel propulsion) are mostly concentrated in California and Colorado. The one standout is SDL — genuinely important work, quietly done, in Logan.

The Utah Test and Training Range (UTTR) in western Utah is unique federal infrastructure enabling reentry testing — Varda Space landed their first in-space manufacturing capsule there in 2024, though they've since shifted to Australia.
