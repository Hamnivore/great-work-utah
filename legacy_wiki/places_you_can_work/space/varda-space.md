# Varda Space Industries

**Tier:** F — not a Utah company; Utah connection is UTTR landing only
**Domain:** Space — in-space manufacturing (pharma crystals in microgravity)
**Type:** Company (private)
**Stage:** Post-revenue (W-1 mission completed 2024; subsequent landings shifting to Australia)
**Location:** El Segundo, CA (not Utah)
**Website:** [varda.com](https://www.varda.com)

California company. The Utah connection: their W-1 re-entry capsule landed at Utah Test and Training Range (UTTR) in 2024. Subsequent missions are going to Australia. The actual insight is interesting: microgravity changes the crystal structure of pharmaceuticals (ritonavir is the canonical example). Growing drug crystals in orbit and landing them could yield better pharmaceutical products. Not a Utah company, but the only non-military user of UTTR's reentry infrastructure, which is worth knowing when thinking about the UTTR entry.
