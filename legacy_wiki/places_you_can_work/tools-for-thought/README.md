# Tools for Thought

Interfaces and environments that genuinely change how humans reason, create, and understand complex things. The bar is high — not productivity apps. Two distinct sub-modes:

- **External augmentation** — live programming environments, direct manipulation interfaces, spatial computing, visualization (à la Bret Victor, Doug Engelbart)
- **Direct neural interfaces** — brain-computer interfaces that bridge cognition and machines

**A note on Utah:** Utah's tools-for-thought story is almost entirely a BCI story. The University of Utah invented the Utah Electrode Array — the most implanted, most studied intracortical BCI in history — and has spun out a cluster of neurotech companies (Blackrock Neurotech, Ripple Neuro, Iris Biomedical, Epitel, Intan Technologies) that are collectively foundational to the global BCI field. The external augmentation space (novel interfaces, live programming environments) has almost no commercial presence in Utah — that work is concentrated in coastal research labs and a handful of companies nationally.
