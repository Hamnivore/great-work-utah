# Transportation

Moving people and things — on the ground, in the air, and eventually to orbit. Includes autonomous vehicles, electric aviation, novel propulsion, and logistics autonomy.

**A note on Utah:** Utah is not a traditional transportation hub, but it has a real electric aviation cluster forming around the 2034 Winter Olympics (Utah was selected as one of eight FAA national testbeds for eVTOL), a strong aerospace supply chain through 47G's 200+ member companies, industrial autonomy work out of Utah State University, and an emerging defense mobility cluster.

## Ecosystem context

- **uFLY / FAA eVTOL testbed** — Utah leading a 5-state FAA pilot program for advanced air mobility. Partners include BETA Technologies, Joby Aviation, Jump Aero, Lockheed Martin. Useful signal for companies to watch as the program develops.
- **47G** — Utah's aerospace & defense ecosystem org. 200+ members. Good pointer to defense-adjacent aerospace companies. [47g.org](https://www.47g.org)
