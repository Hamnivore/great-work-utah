# Jump Aero

**Tier:** F — not a Utah company; interesting EMS eVTOL application
**Domain:** Transportation — eVTOL for emergency medical services
**Type:** Company (private)
**Stage:** Development (JA1 Pulse in development as of 2025)
**Location:** Petaluma, CA (not Utah)
**Website:** Unknown

California company (Petaluma). Founded by Carl Dietrich (previously founded Terrafugia, the flying car company). Building the JA1 Pulse: a single-seat eVTOL designed for emergency medical response — ALS-level first response to remote areas at eVTOL speed. The Utah connection is coverage planning with UDOT as part of the uFLY FAA eVTOL testbed. Not Utah-based, but the EMS use case is one of the most genuinely compelling applications for eVTOL (time-to-care in remote areas is a real life-or-death constraint), more so than air taxis.
