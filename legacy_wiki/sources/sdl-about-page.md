## Summary

SDL's about page describes the laboratory as a nonprofit owned by Utah State University, with UARC status, more than 1,300 employees, and core competencies in sensor systems, instruments, payloads, modular architectures, autonomous systems, stabilization and pointing, and large-scale data processing.

## Useful Claims

- SDL says it is a nonprofit organization owned by Utah State University.
- SDL says it has more than 1,300 employees located at offices nationwide.
- The page describes SDL's UARC role and core competencies.
- Related SDL pages describe North Logan as the headquarters location and the lab's history from earlier Utah space-research labs.
