# greatutah.work — master index

Three layers. Facts describe; judgment recommends; derived indexes are generated from the fact pages and are always current.

## Fact layer

- [ventures](/wiki/indexes/ventures.md) — 117 companies, labs, initiatives (enriched: focus + needs + confidence per line)
- [resources](/wiki/indexes/resources.md) — 218 grants, accelerators, facilities, capital paths
- [people](/wiki/indexes/people.md) — 12 founders, researchers, operators
- [helpers](/wiki/indexes/helpers.md) — 12 advisors, funds, service providers
- [work](/wiki/indexes/work.md) — 34 historical proofs of what Utah has built
- [sources](/wiki/indexes/sources.md) — 203 public evidence records

## Judgment layer

- [guides](/wiki/indexes/guides.md) — 7 opinionated maps and playbooks; start with [find-meaningful-work](/wiki/guides/find-meaningful-work.md) to see how this wiki argues a recommendation

## Derived indexes (generated)
