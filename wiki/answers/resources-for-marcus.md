> **Synthetic persona.** Marcus is a composite test persona: a 34-year-old Ogden veteran leaving the Marines after logistics and manufacturing experience, starting a custom-fabrication and small-batch manufacturing business. He is not a real person.

## Short Answer

Marcus should start with [Utah SBDC](../resources/utah-sbdc.md) for plain-language business planning, then add [Utah MEP](../resources/utah-mep.md) when the question becomes production, quality, compliance, or scaling a real shop. For veteran-specific support, route to [Veteran Business Resource Center (VBRC) Utah](../resources/veteran-business-resource-center-vbrc-utah.md) and [STRIVE](../resources/strive.md). [Startup Ogden](../resources/startup-ogden.md) and [Weber County Economic Development](../resources/weber-county-economic-development.md) add local Ogden context.

## Why

Marcus has skill and execution discipline but may not have startup-network instincts. His questions split early: "Is this a viable business?" and "How do I run a manufacturing operation that does not fall apart at scale?" Those are different support paths. Generic accelerator hype is a weak first fit; practical counseling plus manufacturing extension is stronger.

Utah's manufacturing story is not only startups. Established industrial work — from firearms design to aerospace materials — has deep roots here. Marcus benefits from seeing that advanced manufacturing is a normal Utah path, not a side quest only software founders take.

## Recommended First Calls

1. **[Utah SBDC](../resources/utah-sbdc.md)** — first stop for viability, business planning, pricing, cash flow, lender readiness, and founder operations without startup jargon.
2. **[Utah MEP](../resources/utah-mep.md)** — strongest verified manufacturing help once there is real production, quality, compliance, workforce, or process-improvement work to do.
3. **[Utah Manufacturers Association](../resources/utah-manufacturers-association.md)** — membership, advocacy, workforce, safety, and peer network for companies entering Utah's manufacturing community.
4. **[Veteran Business Resource Center (VBRC) Utah](../resources/veteran-business-resource-center-vbrc-utah.md)** — veteran-focused advising and business-plan support; verify current services on the official site.
5. **[STRIVE](../resources/strive.md)** — six-week veteran entrepreneurship training through VBRC when Marcus wants structured cohort support around an early-stage idea.
6. **[Startup Ogden](../resources/startup-ogden.md)** — Ogden coworking and accelerator space tied to Weber State; useful for local community and workspace.
7. **[Weber County Economic Development](../resources/weber-county-economic-development.md)** — county-level economic development context for Ogden and Weber County.
