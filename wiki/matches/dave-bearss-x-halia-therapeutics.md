## Why This Match Makes Sense

Bearss's public profile sits exactly at the academic-to-venture boundary: senior managing director of the University of Utah Therapeutic Accelerator Hub and founder behind Halia Therapeutics. Halia, in turn, is one of the clearest venture outputs of that world: a Lehi-based clinical-stage biopharma company targeting chronic inflammation through an allosteric NEK7 approach.

The value of the match is not just "founder founded company." It is the combination of drug-discovery credibility, University of Utah translational infrastructure, and an actual clinical-stage program that gives other Utah therapeutics founders a concrete model to study.
