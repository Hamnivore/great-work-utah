## Recommendation

Florian Solzbacher and Ripple Neuro belong together in the wiki's Utah neuroengineering map. Solzbacher represents the commercial BCI company-building path from the University of Utah's Utah Array lineage through Blackrock Neurotech. Ripple represents the research-instrumentation path from the same regional neural-engineering base: multi-channel neural recording, stimulation, and closed-loop neuromodulation systems for neuroscience labs and clinical research programs.

The practical recommendation is for readers to study these pages together when evaluating Utah as a serious neurotechnology cluster, especially when the question is how university research becomes durable companies.

## Why This Match Makes Sense

Solzbacher's public record is about commercializing implantable neural-interface hardware from a university research base. Blackrock Neurotech took the Utah Electrode Array into a global BCI research and clinical ecosystem, with public coverage describing more than 30,000 cumulative patient-days and FDA Breakthrough Device designation for MoveAgain.

Ripple Neuro sits adjacent to that history. Its official website describes neural recording and stimulation systems, including Grapevine, Trek, and Summit, used by research neuroscience and clinical research programs. Ripple's page frames the company as another University of Utah spinout, operational since 2004, building the instruments that make invasive neuroscience experiments possible.

Together, the pages show two sides of the same cluster: implantable interfaces and the recording/stimulation infrastructure around them.

## What Florian Solzbacher Appears To Offer

Solzbacher appears most relevant as a person who has lived the commercialization path for neural-interface hardware: university spinout formation, active implantable-device complexity, FDA-facing clinical translation, and long-horizon BCI ecosystem building. The wiki does not verify his current advisory availability or operational role after Tether Evo's majority stake in Blackrock.

## What Ripple Neuro Appears To Offer

Ripple offers a complementary product surface: research-grade electrophysiology hardware, wireless neural recording, stimulation systems, and neuromodulation research infrastructure. Its official source claims use in major DARPA and DoD-funded neural-engineering programs and NIH HEAL-related neuromodulation work. For a cluster map, Ripple shows that Utah's neurotechnology strength is not one company but a toolchain.

## Risks and Unknowns

- Solzbacher's current role after Blackrock's Tether Evo transaction is unresolved in the wiki and should be verified before making direct asks.
- The evidence base is strong for broad positioning but not sufficient for clinical efficacy claims.
