## Recommendation

Halia Therapeutics is developing ofirnoflast, a first-in-class oral allosteric NEK7 inhibitor, against a well-validated but difficult-to-drug target — a fact pattern where strong composition-of-matter and methods patents are critical to long-term enterprise value. Workman Nydegger, as the Intermountain West's largest IP boutique with published depth in pharma and biotech, is a natural candidate to evaluate for Halia's patent needs.

## Why This Match Makes Sense

A clinical-stage biotech built around a novel mechanism of action lives and dies on patent protection. For Halia, the key asset is both a specific compound (ofirnoflast, a distinct allosteric binder of NEK7) and a novel method of treating NLRP3-driven disease through that mechanism. Composition-of-matter patents on the compound itself and method-of-treatment claims covering its use are the core of any pharma IP estate at this stage.

Workman Nydegger publicly lists pharma/biotech and medical/life-science as practice areas and describes full-service IP covering prosecution, portfolio management, and international filing. For a Utah-based company with a genuinely novel small molecule and a growing pipeline, a Utah-connected boutique that has already navigated pharma prosecution is the right type of firm to explore — even if the company eventually also engages a national firm for the largest filing campaigns.

The match is strongest for Halia's current-stage needs: getting early patent protection around ofirnoflast's structure, mechanism, and method of use in place before expanded clinical data are public.

## What Halia Therapeutics Needs

Based on what is publicly known about Halia's clinical program and pipeline:

- **Composition-of-matter patents** covering ofirnoflast (HT-6184) and structurally related compounds, including any crystalline forms or prodrugs.
- **Method-of-treatment patents** covering use in MDS, obesity/metabolic disease, neuroinflammation, and the broader "inflammaging" indication landscape the company is pursuing.
- **Mechanism patents** on the allosteric NEK7-NLRP3 inhibition approach itself, if the mechanistic claims are novel enough to support an independent claim set.
- **International prosecution**, particularly PCT filings to preserve rights in key pharma markets as the program approaches Phase 2 and 3 data.
- **Portfolio management** that anticipates future pipeline candidates (HT-4253 and any semaglutide combination work) without creating gaps that competitors can exploit.

Halia is private and clinical-stage; cost and efficient prosecution scoping likely matter more here than for a large public company.

## What Workman Nydegger Appears To Offer

Based on the firm's public service descriptions:

- Patent prosecution and portfolio management in pharma/biotech and life sciences — directly relevant to Halia's small-molecule estate.
- International prosecution — important as ofirnoflast moves toward confirmatory trials.
- Trade-secret strategy — relevant for know-how around formulation and manufacturing process not captured in patents.
- Post-grant proceedings — relevant if composition-of-matter claims are challenged.
- The firm reports IAM Patent 1000 recognition (Utah) and Chambers USA (2026) acknowledgment — independent reputational signals specific to the patent practice.

The firm also describes its cost model as customized per client, which is a useful signal for a clinical-stage biotech managing a lean legal budget before a large financing round.

No public source confirms the firm has represented Halia or any of its compounds. This assessment is based on capability overlap from public sources only.

## Risks and Unknowns

- **Pharma prosecution depth**: Workman Nydegger lists pharma/biotech as a practice area but the wiki has not independently verified which specific attorneys handle composition-of-matter or mechanism-of-action patents, or how many pharma prosecution matters they handle annually. Halia should ask for relevant experience in small-molecule prosecution before engaging.
- **Conflicts**: An active Utah biotech IP practice may already represent companies with NLRP3 or inflammasome-adjacent programs. A formal conflicts check is required.
- **International prosecution bandwidth**: PCT and multi-jurisdiction prosecution for a clinical-stage drug can be demanding. The firm's scale and international relationships should be confirmed before assigning primary international prosecution.
- **Stage-fit**: Patent strategy for a Phase 2a program moving toward pivotal data is more time-sensitive and prosecution-intensive than for a preclinical asset. Halia should confirm the firm can meet aggressive timelines.

## Suggested Next Step

A first meeting should establish: (1) whether the firm can clear conflicts given Halia's mechanism and indication space; (2) what specific attorneys would handle pharma prosecution and what relevant experience they bring; and (3) what a scoped engagement for composition-of-matter and method-of-treatment prosecution around ofirnoflast would cost and how long it would take. That scoping call determines whether Workman Nydegger is a primary counsel candidate or a supplementary Utah-based relationship.
