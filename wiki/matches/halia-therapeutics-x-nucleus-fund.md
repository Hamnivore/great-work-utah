## Recommendation

Halia Therapeutics is a Utah clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company with University of Utah ecosystem ties and a mechanism that could become a platform asset. Nucleus Fund publicly targets early-stage equity capital for Utah deep-tech ventures emerging from universities and research institutions, with life sciences among its stated focus areas. The overlap — a research-backed Utah life-sciences company that will need sustained capital and commercialization support — makes Nucleus Fund worth a fit conversation, with the important caveat that Halia may already be past the fund's typical early-stage check size and stage.

## Why This Match Makes Sense

Halia's lead program, ofirnoflast (HT-6184), is a first-in-class oral allosteric NEK7 inhibitor with reported Phase 2a MDS data and a broader pipeline spanning inflammation, obesity/T2D combination work, and neuroinflammation. That is exactly the kind of long-horizon, IP-heavy, technically ambitious company Utah's university commercialization ecosystem produces — and the kind Nucleus Fund says it exists to back.

Founder Dave Bearss has public ties to the University of Utah therapeutics-accelerator community, linking Halia to the same academic drug-discovery network Nucleus Fund is designed to serve. Nucleus Fund's public positioning emphasizes not only capital but mentorship, networks, commercialization resources, and operator matching — relevant for a relatively small clinical-stage team carrying unusually high leverage across discovery, clinical, and regulatory work.

## What Halia Therapeutics Needs

Based on the public venture page, Halia likely needs:

- Later-stage equity capital to move from Phase 2a toward confirmatory studies and broader pipeline execution.
- Crossover and life-sciences investors comfortable with clinical risk and platform-mechanism valuation.
- Regulatory leaders experienced in oncology and hematology trials, plus clinical-operations scale.
- Manufacturing and CDMO partners for small-molecule scale-up as programs advance.
- Strategic support around IP diligence, partnership discussions, and board-level capital planning.

## What Nucleus Fund Appears To Offer

From public sources, Nucleus Fund describes:

- Early-stage equity investment for Utah deep-tech ventures emerging from universities and research institutions.
- Focus areas that include biotechnology and life sciences alongside AI, advanced manufacturing, energy, defense, and other emerging technologies.
- Mentorship, networks, and commercialization resources beyond the check itself.
- Operator matching and connections to legal, financial, marketing, and other service support.
- An application path through the fund's official dealflow portal.

## Risks and Unknowns

- Halia is clinical-stage with reported Phase 2a data — likely further along than the typical "first equity round after proof of concept" company Nucleus Fund may prioritize. Stage and check-size fit should be confirmed before investing preparation time.
- Secondary coverage has cited a Series C and roughly $56M raised; that figure is not confirmed from a primary source in the wiki and should be verified. If accurate, Nucleus Fund may be the wrong capital layer for Halia's next raise.
- Life-sciences crossover investors with clinical-trial diligence depth may matter more than a general deep-tech fund for Halia's immediate financing needs.
- Nucleus Fund's current check-size ranges, target ownership, and diligence timeline are not public; eligibility for a specific company cannot be inferred from the website alone.
- This page does not imply Nucleus Fund has reviewed Halia, agreed to invest, or that any relationship currently exists.

## Suggested Next Step

A Halia founder or finance lead would review Nucleus Fund's current application criteria and stage expectations on the official site, then — if the stage window appears plausible — submit a brief fit inquiry through the dealflow portal highlighting the U of U ecosystem connection, the NEK7 platform mechanism, and the specific capital use (e.g., Phase 2b/3 planning or pipeline expansion) before preparing a full deck.
