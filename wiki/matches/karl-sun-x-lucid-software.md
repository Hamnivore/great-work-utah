## Why This Match Makes Sense

Sun co-founded Lucid Software with Ben Dilts after both graduated from BYU, then led the company behind Lucidchart and Lucidspark through large-scale enterprise adoption and a reported $100 million Series E at a valuation above $3 billion. Lucid's venture page frames the company as one of Utah's flagship examples of visual-collaboration software at scale.

The pairing is a strong judgment-layer artifact because the person page and venture page reinforce the same operating lesson: Utah can produce durable B2B SaaS companies that sell globally while keeping a meaningful local employment and talent footprint.
