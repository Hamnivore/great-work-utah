## Summary

Official U.S. Army article announcing the start of Short-Range Reconnaissance Tranche 2 production, naming Teal Drones and Black Widow as one of two selected manufacturing vendors. Confirms that Tranche 1 fielding began in September 2022 and had reached more than 16 brigades by August 2025, with lessons incorporated into Tranche 2 design.

## Useful Claims

- Army began SRR Tranche 2 production in August 2025
- Teal Drones / Black Widow selected as one of two production vendors
- Tranche 1 fielding reached 16+ brigades by August 2025, starting September 2022
- SRR systems integrate with Uncrewed Vehicle Control and Android Tactical Assault Kit for real-time ISR, target acquisition, threat detection, and route awareness
- Teal is based in Salt Lake City, UT
