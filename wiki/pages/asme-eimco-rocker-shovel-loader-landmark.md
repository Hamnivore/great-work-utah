## Summary

The ASME Historic Mechanical Engineering Landmark designation for the EIMCO Model 12B Rocker Shovel Loader, with the landmark example located in Park City, Utah. The designation page and associated brochure credit EIMCO Corporation of Salt Lake City and describe the machine's significance in mechanizing underground hard-rock mining.

## Useful Claims

- ASME designates the Model 12B as the first successful mining device to replace human labor in removing rubble from underground hard-rock blasting
- The machine was manufactured by EIMCO Corporation in Salt Lake City
- The landmark example is in Park City, Utah
- The rocker-shovel mechanism used compressed air and a bucket-on-rocker-arms design
- About 29,000 units sold by 1969, with licensed manufacturing in Great Britain, India, South Africa, and Japan
