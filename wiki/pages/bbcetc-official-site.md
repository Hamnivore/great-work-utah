## Summary

BBCetc's official site describes a consulting and training firm that helps companies and entrepreneurial-support organizations navigate federal nondilutive R&D funding, especially SBIR/STTR and related government programs.

## Useful Claims

- The site says BBCetc helps companies and support organizations navigate federal funding for technology growth.
- The services navigation lists research grant assistance, SBIR/STTR training, post-award grants/contracts management, programs for entrepreneurial support organizations, TABA, and CMMC.
- The site says SBIR/STTR and other government-program funding is competitive and complex, and positions the firm around proposal assistance, expert training, and experienced guidance.
- The footer lists BBC Entrepreneurial Training & Consulting LLC in Chelsea, Michigan.
