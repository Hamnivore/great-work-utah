## Summary

Verified on the official site (2026-07-14): The Bear Lake Valley Chamber of Commerce unites business leaders across the Bear Lake Valley to build relationships, improve business prosperity, and support the local economy. Public pages highlight networking, member promotion, website advertising, ribbon cuttings, and access to a region that draws more than one million visitors annually.

## Who It Helps

Business owners and operators in the Bear Lake Valley (Rich County, Utah and adjacent Idaho communities) who want local chamber networking, visibility, and tourism-linked promotion.

## What It Provides

Membership benefits listed on the official site include networking, business highlights, website advertising, ribbon cuttings, partnerships, a member directory, and chamber events. The chamber emphasizes collaboration among Bear Lake Valley businesses and tourism-driven economic activity.

Paid chamber membership; apply through the official site. Serves Bear Lake Valley businesses.

## Best Fits

Tourism, hospitality, retail, and service businesses around Bear Lake seeking local networking and chamber promotion — not statewide venture capital or SBIR support.
