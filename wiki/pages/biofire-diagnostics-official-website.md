## Summary

BioFire's official websites and product labeling (biofiredx.com and the BioFire Defense product pages, plus bioMérieux's BioFire panel information sheets) describing the FilmArray syndromic-testing platform and the panel menu, including the FDA-cleared Global Fever Panel.

## Useful Claims

- BioFire Diagnostics, LLC is part of bioMérieux and lists a Salt Lake City address (515 Colorow Drive, Salt Lake City, UT 84108) on product labeling.
- The FilmArray platform (FilmArray 2.0 and Torch systems) runs self-contained multiplex-PCR pouches for syndromic infectious-disease testing.
- The BioFire Global Fever Panel is an FDA-cleared IVD detecting six fever-causing targets (including dengue, chikungunya, *Leptospira*, and *Plasmodium* species) directly from EDTA whole blood in about 50 minutes with roughly two minutes of hands-on time; labeling references a De Novo clearance (DEN200043).
- A research-use-only (RUO) Global Fever Panel detects a broader set of targets for surveillance and research.
- The published panel menu spans respiratory, gastrointestinal, blood culture identification, meningitis/encephalitis, and pneumonia testing.
