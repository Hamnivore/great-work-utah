## Summary

BioHive is a public-private partnership and grassroots community that brands and convenes Utah's life sciences and healthcare innovation ecosystem. Its official site focuses on talent, investment visibility, events such as BioHive Live, and sub-communities including health tech, women in tech and science, returnships, and student chapters.

## Who It's For

Life-sciences and health-innovation founders, operators, students, and ecosystem partners across Utah who need community, visibility, talent connections, and industry events.

## What It Provides

- Ecosystem branding and convening for Utah life sciences
- Events including BioHive Live
- Sub-communities for health tech, women in tech/science, returnships, and students
- Partnership with [BIO Utah](bio-utah.md) on industry community building

## Cost

Public event and community participation costs vary by program. Verify current BioHive Live and membership paths on the official site.

## Best Fits

Utah biotech, medtech, digital health, and life-sciences operators who need ecosystem peers rather than generic startup incubation. Pair with [BIO Utah](bio-utah.md) for trade-association advocacy and [Altitude Labs](altitude-labs.md) for accelerator programming.
