## Summary

The Carbon County Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to fostering economic growth and community development in Carbon County, Utah. It serves businesses of all sizes through initiatives like networking events, promotional opportunities, and advocacy at the local, state, and federal levels, working closely with local cities and the county’s economic development department. Members benefit from resources such as a business directory, training programs, and events like luncheons and award ceremonies, all aimed at enhancing business visibility and community engagement.
