## Summary

Culmination Bio's official website describes a biotech data platform built from Intermountain Health's longitudinal patient records, organized into searchable discovery cohorts for pharma and technology partners.

## Useful Claims

- Culmination says it partners with technology and biopharmaceutical companies to discover health insights and implement findings in diagnostics and therapeutics.
- The site describes a data lake of de-identified patient records spanning more than 40 years, with longitudinal clinical, omics, diagnostic, therapeutic, claims, and EMR data.
- Culmination says data was collected over 40+ years by partner Intermountain Health, which operates 33 hospitals and 385 clinics across nine states in the Intermountain West.
- The site says the partnership includes regular data updates, prospective patient enrollment, and access to more than 9 million archival clinical tissue samples.
- Discovery Cohort data is organized in Apex, described as Culmination's searchable Intelligence Platform, where partners can build custom cohorts.
- The site advertises partner access to disease summaries, diagnosis counts and types, demographics, prescription drugs, and patient-specific genomic overviews within cohorts.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official company source. It is useful for platform positioning, data-scope claims, and partnership structure, but data quality, representativeness, regulatory posture, and commercial traction should be cross-checked with Intermountain Health announcements, investor disclosures, and independent reporting before being treated as high-confidence evidence.
