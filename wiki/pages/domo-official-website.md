## Summary

Domo's official website describes a cloud-native business intelligence and data integration platform that unifies data from hundreds of sources, delivers dashboards and embedded analytics, and layers natural-language and agentic AI capabilities through Domo.AI.

## Useful Claims

- Domo positions itself as a single platform to unify, visualize, and act on business data in real time, replacing fragmented BI stacks.
- The site describes connectors to hundreds of data sources — databases, spreadsheets, and SaaS applications — with data pipeline automation and low-code app-building tools.
- Domo.AI is presented as an agentic analytics layer where business users can query data in natural language, generate dashboards, and build AI agents against their organization's data.
- The company serves midsize to large enterprises across industries including retail, financial services, healthcare, and manufacturing.
- Domo is headquartered in American Fork, Utah; the company was founded in 2010 and trades on NASDAQ under ticker DOMO (IPO 2018).
