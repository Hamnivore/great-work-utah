## Summary

EPIC Ventures, founded in 1994 in Salt Lake City, Utah, is a venture capital firm specializing in early-stage technology investments across sectors such as software-as-a-service (SaaS), healthcare, biotech, security, fintech, and the future of work. The firm has a history of successful investments, including companies like Ancestry, Instructure, Health Catalyst, and Recursion Pharmaceuticals. In September 2024, EPIC Ventures partnered with the University of Utah to launch University of Utah Ventures, Powered by EPIC, aiming to drive regional innovation by supporting early-stage technology companies.
