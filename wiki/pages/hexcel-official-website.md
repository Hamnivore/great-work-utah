## Summary

Hexcel's official website describes the company as a global leader in advanced composite materials across aerospace, defense and space, and industrial applications.

## Useful Claims

- The site lists core product categories including carbon fiber, reinforcements, honeycomb, prepregs, adhesives, resins, and tooling.
- Hexcel says it has 75-plus years serving aerospace, 18 global manufacturing sites, about 5.8k employees worldwide, and $1.89B in 2025 sales.
- The site says Hexcel is the world's largest producer of aerospace carbon fiber and a leading supplier of carbon fiber to U.S. military programs.
- The site says Hexcel provides composites for more than 100 space and defense programs.
