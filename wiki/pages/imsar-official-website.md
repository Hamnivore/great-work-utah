## Summary

IMSAR's official website describes a Utah Valley radar company that designs, builds, and supports low-SWaP, multimode airborne radar systems for manned and unmanned platforms.

## Useful Claims

- IMSAR says it was founded in 2004 and is privately owned.
- The company says it designs, builds, and supports security and intelligence products using synthetic aperture radar technology.
- IMSAR says it works with agencies including the U.S. Department of Defense and Department of Homeland Security.
- The website says IMSAR is based in a 60,000-square-foot Utah Valley facility that includes corporate offices, R&D, and in-house manufacturing.
- IMSAR lists radar systems for Group 1-5 UAS and manned aircraft, plus processing solutions.
- Public capabilities include SAR imaging, change detection, moving target indication, border patrol, and wide-area maritime search.
- The company says it has maintained AS9100D certification since 2018.
