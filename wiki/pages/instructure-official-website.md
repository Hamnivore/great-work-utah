## Summary

Instructure's official website describes Canvas LMS and related products for higher education, K–12, and corporate learning, along with company news and press releases covering ownership transitions and product direction.

## Useful Claims

- Instructure positions itself as a "learning ecosystem" centered on Canvas LMS.
- The site describes products including Canvas LMS, Mastery Connect, and Canvas Credentials.
- Press releases on the site document the July 2024 definitive agreement and November 2024 closing of KKR and Dragoneer's $4.8 billion take-private acquisition.
- Company press materials state Instructure remains headquartered in Salt Lake City after the 2024 transaction.
- The site describes AI-related product investments integrated into the Canvas workflow for tutoring, feedback, and instructor tooling.
