## Summary

Intan Technologies' official website describes specialized integrated circuits and complete electrophysiology systems for neural recording and stimulation, used by biomedical companies and research labs worldwide.

## Useful Claims

- Intan develops specialized integrated circuits to interface with electrical signals in living tissue and create microchips to sense and stimulate neurons and muscles.
- Product families include RHD recording systems, RHS stim/recording systems, RHX data acquisition software, and standalone amplifier chips (RHD, RHA, RHS families).
- The site says Intan products are used by biomedical companies and research labs in over 50 countries.
- As of 2026, Intan is no longer directly selling controllers, headstages, cables, or adapters; White Mountain Systems serves as the primary worldwide distributor for complete recording and stimulation systems. Microchip purchases remain direct through Intan.
- The company tagline is "Electrophysiology, miniaturized."
