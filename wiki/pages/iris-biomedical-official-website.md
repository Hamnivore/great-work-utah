## Summary

Iris Biomedical's official website describes contract design and manufacturing services for custom neural interface hardware, including implantable electrode arrays, flexible substrates, hermetic packages, and connectors for academic research and commercial neurotech clients.

## Useful Claims

- Iris Biomedical offers contract design, engineering, and manufacturing for custom neural interface devices.
- The company specializes in implantable electrode arrays, flexible substrates, and hermetic packaging.
- Iris Biomedical serves academic neuroscience labs and early-stage commercial neurotech companies that lack in-house fabrication capability.
- The company is associated with the University of Utah neural engineering ecosystem (the site emerged from that community).
- Core competencies include thin-film microfabrication, hermetic sealing, and biocompatibility testing.
