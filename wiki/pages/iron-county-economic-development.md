## Summary

Cedar City's Economic Development office promotes business growth, company attraction, and quality-of-life improvements in Iron County. Verified on the official city site (2026-07-14): the page highlights regional assets (transportation, air cargo, highway access), publishes business news, and links to a Resimplifi property search map for available commercial sites around Cedar City.

This is a municipal economic-development front door, not a standalone loan fund.

## Who It Helps

Businesses expanding in or relocating to Cedar City/Iron County, especially growth-stage employers and site selectors evaluating southern Utah. Also useful to local leaders tracking development news and available properties.

## What It Provides

Economic-development marketing and regional data, news on major local projects, and a linked commercial property search tool (Utah Resimplifi map centered on Cedar City). Staff support for business attraction and expansion inquiries is implied through the city department channel.

## Cost / Eligibility

City economic-development outreach is generally free to businesses; any incentives or fee-based services depend on specific city/county programs not detailed on the landing page. Contact the department for expansion or relocation questions.

## Best Fits

Employers and site selectors evaluating Cedar City for expansion, especially when property discovery and regional momentum data are the immediate need.

Less useful for pre-revenue founders seeking accelerator mentorship or equity capital.
