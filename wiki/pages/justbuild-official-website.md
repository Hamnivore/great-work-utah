## Summary

The official JustBuild website describes an open Utah community organized around AI building, a Slack workspace, hackathons, and recurring coworking sessions. The events page provides dated examples of past events and participation.

## Useful Claims

- JustBuild describes its mission as making Utah AI-native and helping Utah founders through events and community.
- The site reports more than 400 builders and more than $100,000 in prizes across its activities.
- Anyone may join without credentials, and an existing company is not required.
- The public call to action links directly to a Slack community.
- Regular coworking sessions are called `saturdays@byu`; the official events calendar describes weekly sessions in Utah County.
- Past events include hackathons where participants formed teams and shipped working projects.

## Reliability Notes

This is the organization's own current description. Membership and prize totals are self-reported, and the public pages do not establish Slack activity, member availability, or a formal cofounder-matching success rate. Event schedules and Slack invitation routes may change.
