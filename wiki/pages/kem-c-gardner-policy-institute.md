## Summary

The Kem C. Gardner Policy Institute, part of the University of Utah's David Eccles School of Business, conducts economic, demographic, and public policy research to inform decision-making and promote Utah's prosperity. Serving as a vital hub for policy leadership, the institute provides data-driven insights on topics such as the economy, public finance, demographics, and public policy. Their work supports state leaders in making informed decisions that benefit all Utahns.
