## Summary

The official history page for Kennecott Utah Copper covers the development of the Bingham Canyon Mine from early twentieth-century origins through current operations. It describes the mine's scale, production history, and the company's role as a subsidiary of Rio Tinto.

## Useful Claims

- Bingham Canyon Mine is the largest open-pit copper mine in the world by volume excavated
- The mine has produced more copper than any other mine in history
- Operations began in 1906 under what became the Kennecott brand
- The mine is designated a National Historic Landmark
- Rio Tinto has invested in extending mine life to 2032 and beyond

## Reliability Notes
