## Summary

LVT's official website describes the company as an American Fork, Utah provider of rapidly deployable, solar-powered mobile surveillance units with cloud-based AI video analytics, sold on a subscription model.

## Useful Claims

- Founded in 2005 by Ryan Porter (CEO) and Bob Brenner; began with construction-site streaming and expanded into remote physical security.
- Products are self-contained mobile units (cameras, solar power, cellular connectivity, lights/strobes/speakers) plus a cloud video-management platform; a wall-mounted unit was added in 2025.
- The September 2022 press release states LVT "secured $135M": a $50M Series B equity round (Sorenson Capital, Larry H. Miller Group, Lead Edge Capital, Pelion Venture Partners) and an $85M debt facility led by KeyBanc Capital Markets and MUFG Bank.
- Headquarters at 802 E 1050 S, American Fork, UT; manufacturing in Spanish Fork, UT.

## Reliability Notes

Official source: reliable for product description, history, locations, and the structure of the 2022 financing, but not neutral for outcome claims (crime-reduction percentages, deterrence effectiveness), which come from company case studies. Deployment counts (15,000+) and headcount (200+) appear in 2025 trade coverage and are date-sensitive. The 2022 $135M figure combines equity and debt and should not be cited as a single equity round.
