## Summary

Maschoff Brennan's official site describes a law firm focused on intellectual property and adjacent corporate, technology-transaction, privacy, data-security, litigation, and regulatory counseling work. The site lists offices in Salt Lake City and Park City, Utah, along with offices in California and New York.
