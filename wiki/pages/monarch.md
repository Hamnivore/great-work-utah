## Summary

The Monarch is a renovated historic creative hub in Ogden's Nine Rails Creative District at 455 25th Street. The official site describes more than 40 independent creative studios, Monarch Venues event space, Ogden Contemporary Arts gallery, Sola salon suites, Weller's Bistro, The Art Box retail/candle studio, and public markets and live-music programming.

## Who It's For

Artists, makers, creative entrepreneurs, salon professionals, and event organizers seeking studio space, gallery exposure, or venue rental in downtown Ogden — as well as visitors attending public markets and arts programming.

## What It Provides

Creative studio memberships, premier event venues, gallery exhibition space, salon suites for independent beauty professionals, retail/creative workshop space, on-site dining, and a public events calendar (open studios, markets, live music).

## Cost

Studio, salon-suite, and venue pricing are not listed as flat public rates on the homepage. Contact Monarch staff or the relevant studio/venue page for current rates.

## Best Fits

Creative-economy founders and arts entrepreneurs who need Ogden studio or event infrastructure and community exposure — less direct for conventional tech coworking needs.
