## Summary

Morgan County Economic Development is the county's portal for business and community growth in Morgan, Utah. Verified on morgancountyutah.gov/economic-development: the page ties economic development to the Morgan County Utah Chamber of Commerce and county government services — the prior Startup State URL (morgancountyinfo.com) pointed to Morgan County, Colorado, not Utah.

## Who It Helps

Businesses and residents in Morgan County seeking a local government contact for economic development and chamber connection; operators evaluating Morgan as a lower-density Wasatch-adjacent location.

## What It Provides

County economic development page with chamber linkage, county government contact path (48 West Young Street, Morgan, UT 84050), and integration with broader Morgan County departments (planning, tourism, recreation).

## How To Access It

Use the official county economic development page and Morgan Area Chamber of Commerce links on morgancountyutah.gov.

- [Official website](https://www.morgancountyutah.gov/economic-development) · https://www.morgancountyutah.gov/economic-development
- County contact form via morgancountyutah.gov
- Startup State listing: https://startup.utah.gov/business-resource/morgan-county-economic-development/

## Cost / Eligibility

No public fees listed on the economic development page. Confirm any chamber membership costs directly with Morgan Area Chamber of Commerce.

## Best Fits

Morgan County brick-and-mortar or small-business operators who need a local county/chamber entry point — not a deep startup stack. Pair with WFRC, Davis/Weber ED, or SBDC for broader programs.
