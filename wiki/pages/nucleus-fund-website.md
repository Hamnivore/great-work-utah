## Summary

The Nucleus Fund website describes the fund as an early-stage investment vehicle for Utah's deep-tech sector, with a focus on ventures emerging from Utah universities and research institutions.

## Useful Claims

- The fund says it invests in early-stage deep-tech ventures emerging from Utah universities and research institutions.
- The site emphasizes mentorship, networks, commercialization resources, and connecting state-institution research to global markets.
- The site provides an "Apply For Funding" path through an external dealflow portal.

## Reliability Notes

This is the fund's official public site. It is useful for current positioning and access path, but it does not by itself establish whether a specific company is eligible, whether capital is available, or whether the fund is interested in a particular venture.
