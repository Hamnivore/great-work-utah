## Summary

The Nucleus programs page lists several Nucleus initiatives, including Nucleus Grow, Nucleus Fund, Nucleus ProLab, Nucleus MarketEdge, Nucleus Catalyst, Nucleus Global, and Nucleus Quantum.

## Useful Claims

- Nucleus Grow supports Utah deep tech, life sciences, AI, advanced manufacturing, cleantech, and frontier-innovation companies.
- Nucleus Fund is described as the reimagined Utah Innovation Fund, embedded within Nucleus, with early-stage capital and strategic support for founders connected with Utah higher education institutions.
- Nucleus MarketEdge is aimed at professors, PhDs, and postdocs in Utah higher education who are exploring commercialization and startup formation.
- Nucleus ProLab connects students from Utah public colleges and universities with companies, nonprofits, and public agencies for short-term applied projects.
