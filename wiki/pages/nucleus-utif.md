## Summary

The UTIF page describes the Utah Technology Innovation Funding program, which supports Utah small companies pursuing SBIR/STTR proposals and awards.

## Useful Claims

- UTIF supports Utah small companies developing novel technology and products through SBIR/STTR.
- Eligible Phase I applicants may seek microgrants after successful Phase I SBIR/STTR proposal submission.
- Eligible Phase I winners may seek nonrecourse loans to bridge the gap between Phase I and Phase II SBIR/STTR R&D.
- The page says applicants must identify a specific open SBIR/STTR opportunity and submit the UTIF application at least four weeks before the related federal deadline.
