## Summary

Parsons Behle & Latimer's official website describes a regional law firm with a Salt Lake City office (plus offices in Idaho, Montana, Wyoming/Jackson, and elsewhere in the Mountain West). Its capability pages cover technology, intellectual property, securities and capital markets, and emerging companies.

## Useful Claims

- The firm maintains a "Technology, Licensing & Intellectual Property Transactions" practice covering patent, trademark, copyright, and trade-secret protection plus licensing, joint-development agreements, and IP due diligence for M&A. [Technology / IP capability pages]
- The Securities & Capital Markets practice says it advises issuers and "startup, middle market, and other emerging growth companies" on equity and debt transactions, "from structuring the early round of angel financing to the more complex rounds of investment fund and bank financing."
- Attorney profiles list practice tags including "Emerging Companies & Venture Capital," and the firm publicly describes representing Utah technology companies in capital financing rounds.
- The firm reports IP-practice recognition (e.g., World Trademark Review's "WTR 1000").
