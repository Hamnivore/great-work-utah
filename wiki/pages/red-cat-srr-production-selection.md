## Summary

Investor-relations press release from Red Cat Holdings (Teal Drones' parent company) announcing production selection for the Army SRR program. Published November 2024. States that Teal's next-generation system won Army SRR production selection after testing by Army UAS, Maneuver Battle Lab, Army Test and Evaluation Command, and Army Operational Test Center.

## Useful Claims

- Teal Drones selected for Army SRR production after competitive evaluation by multiple Army testing organizations
- Testing included Army UAS, Maneuver Battle Lab, Army Test and Evaluation Command, and Army Operational Test Center
- Teal Drones manufactures in Salt Lake City, UT
- Black Widow described as Blue UAS certified with secure communications and modular repair
