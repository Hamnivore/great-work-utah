## Summary

The Revolving Loan Fund (RLF), administered by the R6 Regional Council, offers "gap financing" to support new and expanding businesses within Juab, Millard, Piute, Sanpete, Sevier, and Wayne counties in Utah. This program provides loans ranging from $20,000 to $250,000, aiming to stimulate economic growth and job creation in the region.
