## Summary

Salt Lake City Angels (SLCA) is an angel investment forum where accredited investors collectively research early-stage companies and independently choose whether to invest capital and mentorship. The official site emphasizes a broad investment thesis without geography, industry, or customer restrictions.

## Who It's For

Early-stage founders seeking angel capital and structured group due diligence. SLCA targets companies that can benefit from a diverse accredited-investor cohort in Salt Lake City.

## What It Provides

- Collective due diligence by accredited angel members
- Potential angel investment and mentorship for selected companies
- Forum for deal flow, member research, and investment decisions

## How To Access

- Contact SLCA through the official site for founder engagement
- [Official website](https://slcangels.org/) · https://slcangels.org/

## Cost

No public application fee is listed. Investment terms are deal-specific if members invest.

## Best Fits

Founders with an early-stage company ready for angel-group review. Not a fit for non-equity capital or companies needing manufacturing or export services.
