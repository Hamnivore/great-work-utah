## Summary

The Salt Lake City Angels (SLCA) is an angel investment group that provides a platform for investors to collaboratively research and evaluate early-stage companies, offering both capital and mentorship to selected startups. SLCA emphasizes core values such as mutual respect, human dignity, ethical business practices, diversity, and humility in all interactions with members and entrepreneurs. Their strategy focuses on a broad investment thesis, fostering a diverse membership of accredited investors, maintaining a robust pipeline of quality startups, conducting comprehensive due diligence, and ensuring effective organizational administration.
