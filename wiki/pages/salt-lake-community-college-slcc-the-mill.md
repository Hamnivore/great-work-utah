## Summary

The Mill at Salt Lake Community College is an entrepreneurship center and business incubator in Sandy. The live site describes workspaces, education, and resource partners for founders from idea stage through scaling, with pathways to work, learn, and network on campus.

## Who It's For

Utah entrepreneurs and SLCC-linked founders at idea, pre-revenue, or revenue stages who want incubator-style support across launching, marketing, bookkeeping, funding, leadership, importing/exporting, sales, scaling, and manufacturing.

## What It Provides

Business incubator programming organized around Work Here (workspace), Learn Here (education), and Network Here (community); intake through a Get Started form that captures business stage and resource needs; and featured client success stories on the public site.

## Cost

The public site does not publish a single fee schedule. Confirm workspace, incubator, and advising costs during intake.

## Best Fits

Salt Lake Valley founders who want a Sandy-based incubator with structured intake and SLCC/SBDC ecosystem ties rather than a pure coworking desk.
