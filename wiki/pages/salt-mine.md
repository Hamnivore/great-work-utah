## Summary

Salt Mine Productive Workspace, located at 7984 S 1300 E in Sandy, UT, launched in 2016 as a home for the start-up, tech, and entrepreneur community of Silicon Slopes. The 20,000sf facility provides flexible community coworking, dedicated desks, private offices, large training and event spaces, a la carte conference rooms all with floor to ceiling whiteboard walls, a built-in coffee shop and is host to many community networking events and the South Valley Chamber of Commerce Business Accelerator Academy.
