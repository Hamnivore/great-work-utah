## Summary

An anonymized firsthand report supplied to greatutah.work by a person who attended Sandbox in 2024. It records the participant's experience of the cohort as a place to find cofounders and a historical price they paid or were quoted as a student unaffiliated with a partner school.

## Useful Claims

- The participant entered a cohort in which people began without a business idea or cofounder and built real businesses during the program.
- In the participant's judgment, the cohort included unusually strong prospective founders in Utah and most participants were actively looking for cofounders.
- The participant compared the experience to a smaller, more accessible version of Y Combinator, while emphasizing that it offered fewer funding opportunities.
- The reported 2024 entry cost for someone unaffiliated with a school was $2,000.

## Reliability Notes

This is one participant's account, not an official price sheet or representative survey. The cohort-quality and Y Combinator comparisons are informed opinions. The $2,000 figure is historical, applies to a particular 2024 affiliation context, and has not been independently verified. Current Sandbox programs use different structures and published tuition; live recommendations must check current terms.

The contributor's identity is intentionally withheld because it is unnecessary to evaluate the claims and was not offered for publication.
