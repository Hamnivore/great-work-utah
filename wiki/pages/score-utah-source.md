## Summary

SCORE Utah describes itself as a statewide small-business support organization that connects entrepreneurs with free, confidential mentoring and education from experienced volunteer business professionals. The page lists mentoring, workshops, events, sponsor partners, and a mentor request path.

## Useful Claims

- SCORE Utah supports small businesses across Utah with free, confidential mentoring and education.
- Entrepreneurs can request a mentor and register for workshops.
- SCORE's national SBA page describes SCORE as a resource partner that helps small businesses plan, launch, manage, and grow.
- The Utah page lists statewide service coverage and local chapter access.

## Reliability Notes

This is appropriate evidence for SCORE Utah's public service model. Mentor availability, fit, and expertise should not be assumed until the user submits the SCORE matching request or identifies a specific mentor profile.
