## Summary

The Sevier County Economic Development Council (SCEDC) facilitates business growth and relocation in Sevier County, Tennessee, by connecting businesses with city planners and providing essential resources. They offer information on industrial parks, planning and permitting processes, and demographic data to assist businesses in making informed decisions. SCEDC also collaborates with educational institutions to support workforce development through programs like the Sevierville Hospitality Internship Program and S.E.V.I.E.R. Ready.
