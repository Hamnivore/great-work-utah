## Summary

The GRIT Center for Rural Entrepreneurship at Snow College, funded by the Utah State Legislature and opened in November 2019, helps students develop leadership skills and an entrepreneurial mindset through experiential programming. The official page highlights pitch competitions, the Entrepreneurship Seminar Series, and the Bootstrap Your Business competition with a published $500 award.

## Who It's For

Snow College students from any major who want rural entrepreneurship exposure, seminar learning, and low-stakes pitch practice — plus community visitors invited to entrepreneurship seminars.

## What It Provides

Entrepreneurship Seminar Series with visiting founders; Bootstrap Your Business pitch competition; pitch events with funding opportunities; entrepreneurial-mindset skill development (problem solving, initiative, opportunity recognition, and related competencies); and GRIT Center contact/support at 435.283.7564.

## Cost

Seminars are open to invited community participants; student participation is the primary pathway. Bootstrap Your Business advertises a $500 award — verify current entry deadlines and rules on the competition page.

## Best Fits

Central Utah students who want rural entrepreneurship programming and small-stakes pitch funding without leaving the Snow College ecosystem.
