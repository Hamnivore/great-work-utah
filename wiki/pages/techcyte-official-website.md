## Summary

Techcyte's official website describes Fusion, a unified anatomic and clinical digital-pathology platform with AI-assisted workflows for surgical pathology, cytology, microbiology, hematology, veterinary diagnostics, and related markets. The company is headquartered in Orem, Utah.

## Useful Claims

- Fusion is positioned as the first unified anatomic and clinical pathology AI platform, spanning tissue and liquid-based workflows.
- Product suite includes Fusion AP, Fusion Frozen Section Suite, Techcyte SureView Cervical Cytology System, and Fusion suites for parasitology, bacteriology, hematology, and organ donation.
- Fusion AP is described as CE-IVDR certified; U.S. regulatory status varies by product (site notes research-use-only positioning for some U.S. offerings).

- Orem, Utah addresses listed: 1106 N 1200 W and 105 S State Street.
