## Summary

Torus's official website describes a full-stack energy platform combining storage, software, cybersecurity, monitoring, flywheel energy storage, and deployment services for utilities, manufacturers, data centers, and other power-sensitive facilities.

## Useful Claims

- Torus says Torus Station unites storage, software, security, and 24/7 monitoring in one platform.
- The company says Torus Spin is a flywheel product that reacts in milliseconds and handles high-power events that can degrade chemical batteries.
- Torus says it was founded in 2021 in Springville, Utah.
- The company says its R&D and manufacturing facilities are across Utah and that every product is built with U.S.-sourced steel.
- The mission page reports a 582,000-square-foot U.S. manufacturing facility, 500 MW in utility partnerships, $267 million raised, and a 2025 R&D 100 award.
- Public customer categories include utilities, manufacturing and industrial, and data centers.
