## Summary

Trace AQ's official website markets physics-based AI air quality forecasting and alerts for healthcare and other customers. Products include the Flow AQ mobile app for consumers, the Trace AQ | Aero forecasting platform for researchers and enterprises, and the ADAPT embeddable air-quality widget for websites.

## Useful Claims

- The company positions itself around "physics-based AI" models that decode atmospheric dynamics for air quality forecasting, with emphasis on wildfire smoke tracking and forecasting.
- Flow AQ is described as providing air quality forecasts, real-time conditions, personalized alerts, and health-driven insights in a single mobile app.
- Forecasts can target any location — not limited to fixed sensor placement — with up to four-day advance air quality forecasting.
- Trace AQ | Aero is marketed as a wildfire smoke and air quality forecasting system with plume-rise forecasting, continuous source detection, four-day forecast play-through, and timelapse graphs and map animations.
- The site advertises dual API access for integration and a researcher discount program for Aero.
- The ADAPT widget is offered for embedding live air-quality forecasts on third-party websites to drive engagement.
- Whitepapers are published on topics including degraded air quality impacts on athletic training and the Air Quality Analytics (AQA) physics-based AI forecast engine.
- Primary customer segment highlighted on the homepage is healthcare ("Air Quality Forecasting & Alerts for HEALTHCARE").
