## Summary

The Utah Asian Chamber of Commerce (UACC) is a registered 501(c)(6) nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting the social and economic interests of Asian professionals and businesses in Utah. UACC offers its members access to exclusive events, business mentorship, and various resources aimed at fostering successful entrepreneurship and professional development. The Chamber organizes events such as the Annual Scholarship & Awards Gala and the Access Asia program to support local Asian communities and businesses.
