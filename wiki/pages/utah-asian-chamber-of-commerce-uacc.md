## Summary

The Utah Asian Chamber of Commerce (UACC) is a 501(c)(6) organization promoting Asian professionals and businesses in Utah. Verified on the official site (2026-07-14): membership includes exclusive events, business mentorship, and resources aimed at entrepreneurship and professional development.

The public site highlights membership enrollment and sponsor visibility more than open grant or loan programs.

## Who It Helps

Asian professionals, entrepreneurs, and Utah businesses seeking chamber networking, mentorship, and community visibility through a multicultural business association.

## What It Provides

Membership access to chamber events, mentorship connections, business resources, and sponsor-backed programming. The homepage directs prospects to membership details and a contact form for joining.

## Cost / Eligibility

Membership details are referenced on the site but specific dues are not published on the homepage. Use the membership page or contact form to confirm pricing and benefits.

## Best Fits

Asian-owned or Asian-serving Utah businesses that want chamber events, mentorship, and professional network access.

Less useful for founders seeking non-dilutive capital, SBIR strategy, or open accelerator cohorts with published curricula.
