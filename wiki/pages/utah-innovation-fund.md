## Summary

The Utah Innovation Fund is a state-established venture capital fund dedicated to elevating Utah's economy by commercializing technologies developed at the state's higher education institutions. Its mission is to support and fund promising startups addressing global challenges, with a focus on early investments in innovative solutions and fostering deep tech leadership within Utah.
