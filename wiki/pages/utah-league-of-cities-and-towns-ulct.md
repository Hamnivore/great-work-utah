## Summary

The Utah League of Cities and Towns (ULCT), established in 1907, is a non-partisan, inter-local government cooperative representing 248 municipalities across Utah. ULCT advocates for municipal interests at state and federal levels, providing information, training, and technical assistance to local officials on various municipal issues. The organization aims to strengthen the quality of local governance and administration throughout Utah's cities and towns.
