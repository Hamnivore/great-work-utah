## Summary

The Utah Muslim Civic League (UMCL) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to educating and empowering Utah's Muslim community to engage in civic and charitable activities. UMCL represents over 60,000 Muslims from 120 nationalities in Utah, with an active voter base exceeding 15,000. Their initiatives include voter education, leadership development, advocacy, and community resource programs, aiming to amplify Muslim voices and foster inclusive civic participation.
