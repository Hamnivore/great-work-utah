## Summary

The Utah Native American Chamber of Commerce, based in Salt Lake City, is dedicated to empowering Native American entrepreneurs and fostering the prosperity of Indigenous businesses across Utah. As a non-profit organization, it provides a platform for Native American entrepreneurs to showcase their businesses, connect with corporations, and serve as a liaison on tribal economic development issues. The Chamber offers resources, training, and networking opportunities tailored to the unique needs of Native American business owners, welcoming both Native and non-Native entrepreneurs to join in this collective journey of empowerment and collaboration.
