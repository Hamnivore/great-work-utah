## Summary

The official Utah Population Database page describes UPDB as a Huntsman Cancer Institute and University of Utah research resource linking Utah family histories, demographic records, medical records, and registry data for genetics, epidemiology, demography, and public-health research.

## Useful Claims

- The page calls UPDB one of the world's richest sources of in-depth information for genetics, epidemiology, demography, and public-health research.
- It says University of Utah and other researchers have used UPDB since the mid-1970s.
- It describes UPDB as linking Utah family histories with demographic and medical information, including cancer diagnoses, causes of death, and birth-associated medical details.
- It says UPDB includes statewide inpatient hospital discharge claims and outpatient/ambulatory surgery records.
- It says UPDB provides access to information on more than 11 million individuals and supports approximately 400 research projects.
- The page states that RGE and an Institutional Review Board oversee access through an independent review process.

## Reliability Notes

This is the official institutional source and is strong for scope, governance framing, record categories, and self-reported scale. It is not sufficient by itself for claims about specific discoveries or representativeness; those should cite peer-reviewed studies using UPDB.
