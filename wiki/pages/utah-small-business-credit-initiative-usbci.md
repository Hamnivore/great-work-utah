## Summary

The Utah Small Business Credit Initiative (USBCI), administered by the Governor's Office of Economic Opportunity, is a statewide program designed to enhance access to capital for small businesses, particularly those in underserved communities.

Summary text refreshed from the live Startup State listing (2026-07-14); verify current products, partners, and eligibility on the official USBCI page before recommending.

## Who It's For

Utah small businesses seeking credit access — especially operators in underserved communities — and lenders/partners participating in USBCI capital-access products. Not a substitute for venture equity; treat as a credit / capital-access pathway.

## How To Use It

1. Read the official program page for current products and partner lenders.
2. Confirm eligibility (business size, location, use of proceeds) before applying through a participating lender.
3. Contact GOEO capital-access staff if the site does not list a clear apply path.
