## Summary

The UVU Innovation Academy is a university-wide hub connecting students, faculty, and external partners through hands-on learning experiences. The official site describes it as home to more than a dozen programs supporting research, graduation distinctions, industry connections, and engaged learning.

## Who It's For

UVU students seeking real-world project experience, faculty building interdisciplinary courses, and external partners (businesses or nonprofits) collaborating with student teams. Primary audience is student-facing; partnership pathways exist for employers and community organizations.

## What It Provides

Student, faculty, and partnership opportunity portals; programs including Career & Internship Center, e2i, Education Abroad, Experience Fellows, Empowered, Excelerate, Faculty Externships, FanX Studios, Huntsman Cancer Institute collaboration, Jobs CEO Council, MajorQuest, Professional Mentorships, Service Learning, Undergraduate Research & Creative Works, and Workforce Alignment.

## Cost

Program costs vary by enrollment, travel, or partnership structure. Many student opportunities are university-sponsored; verify any fees on the specific program page before recommending.

## Best Fits

UVU students who want project-based entrepreneurship and industry exposure rather than a standalone incubator desk. External companies seeking student talent or capstone partnerships. A weaker fit for non-UVU founders needing immediate free SBDC-style advising.
