## Summary

Varda's official website describes an in-space manufacturing company that develops platforms to grow materials — especially pharmaceutical crystals — in microgravity and return products to Earth in recoverable reentry capsules. The company is headquartered in El Segundo, California.

## Useful Claims

- Varda's thesis is that microgravity enables superior crystal growth for certain pharmaceuticals and advanced materials.
- The company builds orbital manufacturing spacecraft integrated with launch partners and small recoverable reentry capsules.
- Public mission materials reference ritonavir as a canonical pharmaceutical example for microgravity crystallization.
- Varda is a commercial space company, not a Utah-headquartered venture; Utah relevance comes from reentry operations (see [varda-uttr-landing](varda-uttr-landing.md)).
