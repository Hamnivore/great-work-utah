## Summary

The Vernal Innovation Hub is a Uintah Basin coworking and incubator at 431 E Main St in Vernal. The official site offers flexible memberships, private offices, dedicated desks, conference rooms, a podcast/multimedia room, and business-development programming including the Rise & Thrive Business Speed Pitch competition.

## Who It's For

Entrepreneurs, remote workers, and small businesses in the Uintah Basin (Daggett, Duchesne, and Uintah counties per Startup State coverage) who need affordable workspace, meeting rooms, or pitch-event exposure.

## What It Provides

Coworking open workspace, dedicated desks, private offices, hourly conference and multimedia rooms, mail handling, high-speed internet, business-development classes, and the annual Rise & Thrive Business Speed Pitch for startups.

## Cost

Published membership rates on the official site: monthly open workspace $49/month; dedicated desk $99/month; private office $199/month (first year) then $299/month (second year); daily drop-in $5/day; private office by day $19.98/day; conference room $10–$20/hour; podcast/multimedia room $5/hour. Confirm current tiers before quoting.

## Best Fits

Uintah Basin founders who want local coworking and pitch exposure without relocating to Salt Lake City. A weaker fit for founders needing deep-tech manufacturing infrastructure or equity investors on site.
