## Summary

The Vernal Innovation Hub offers coworking spaces and private offices for entrepreneurs and small businesses, providing essential services like high-speed internet, mail handling, and business development classes. It also hosts the annual "Rise & Thrive Business Speed Pitch" competition to support startups, helping them grow and eventually transition into commercial spaces​.
