## Summary

Vivint's official website markets professionally installed smart home security systems — hardware, monitoring, installation, and financing — to U.S. residential customers. The site positions Vivint as a leading smart home security company with integrated cameras, sensors, hubs, automation, and 24/7 professional monitoring.

## Useful Claims

- Vivint describes itself as "America's most trusted smart home security company" and emphasizes professional installation, 24/7 monitoring, and customizable packages.
- Product families include the Vivint Smart Hub / Smart Hub Pro 2, door and window sensors, motion sensors, indoor and outdoor cameras, Doorbell Camera Pro, smart locks, garage control, thermostats, and safety alarms — controlled through the Vivint app.
- The site highlights "Smart Deter" technology on cameras, flexible financing (including 0% APR tiers on qualifying orders), and month-to-month monitoring options on select online offers.
- Standard packages (e.g., HomeProtect) include defined equipment bundles, professional installation fees, and recurring monitoring starting around $29.99–$44.99/month depending on package — subject to promotional terms.
- Vivint states it provides services under state-specific licensing; Louisiana operations reference Vivint Louisiana Commercial Certificate #58280.
