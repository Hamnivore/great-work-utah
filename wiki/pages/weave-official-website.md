## Summary

Weave's official website and investor materials describe an AI-powered patient communications, engagement, and payments platform purpose-built for small and medium-sized healthcare practices. The site and SEC filings document the product suite, customer base, target verticals, and AI feature set.

## Useful Claims

- Weave describes itself as a leading AI-powered patient communications, engagement, and payments platform for SMB healthcare practices, operating as an "orchestration layer" across voice and text.
- Product suite includes a unified phone system, AI Receptionist, text messaging and response assistants, reviews, email marketing, digital forms, online scheduling, insurance eligibility/verification, Call Intelligence, practice analytics, and Weave Payments.
- Supported verticals are Dental, Optometry, Veterinary, and Specialty Medical (described as 29 specialties and the second-largest, fastest-growing vertical by location count).
- Reported nearly 40,000 customer locations and more than 30,000 customers in the US and Canada as of December 31, 2025, with no single customer over 5% of revenue.
- Headquartered at 1331 W Powell Way, Lehi, Utah; trades on NYSE as WEAV; founded 2008, renamed Weave Communications in 2015; references a TrueLark acquisition.

## Reliability Notes

The marketing site is reliable for product names and positioning; the customer counts, verticals, and corporate facts cited here are drawn from Weave's SEC filings (FY2025 Form 10-K and Q4/FY2025 earnings release), which are higher-confidence than marketing copy. Forward-looking statements and "leading" positioning language remain promotional and should not be treated as competitive proof.
