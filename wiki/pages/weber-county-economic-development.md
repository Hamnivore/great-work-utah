## Summary

The Weber Economic Development Partnership (EDP) focuses on fostering economic growth and development in Weber County by supporting businesses and attracting new investments. It offers resources, strategic planning, and workforce development programs to strengthen the local economy. Through collaboration and innovation, Weber EDP promotes the county as a dynamic location for businesses and residents.
