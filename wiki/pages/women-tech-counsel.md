## Summary

The Women Tech Council (WTC) is a national organization with deep Utah roots that amplifies the economic impact of women in technology through programs spanning high school through executive leadership. Its site describes mentoring, visibility, networking, research, and signature initiatives including SheTech and the Women Tech Awards.

## Who It's For

Women and allies in Utah tech — from high-school students exploring STEM through executives and founders building inclusive companies.

## What It Provides

Public programs include SheTech (high-school STEM activation), the Women Tech Awards, Innovation Summit events, research reports on women in tech leadership and workplace flexibility, corporate inclusion programming, partner events, and a community the site describes as 10,000+ professionals. Contact email on site: info@womentechcouncil.org.

## Cost

Event and program costs vary; many resources and reports are public. Verify registration fees for summits, SheTech, or awards programs on the official site.

## Best Fits

Strong fit for Utah women in tech seeking mentorship, visibility, STEM pipeline programs (SheTech), or executive peer networks.

Weaker fit for founders whose primary need is dilutive funding term sheets or wet-lab biotech incubation.
