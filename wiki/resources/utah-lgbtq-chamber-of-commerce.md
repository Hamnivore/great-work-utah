## Summary

The Utah LGBTQ+ Chamber of Commerce supports LGBTQ+ and allied businesses by fostering an inclusive business community across the state. Founded in 2012 and expanded in 2019 to reflect greater inclusivity, it connects businesses, nonprofits, and government entities to promote equity, diversity, and inclusion. The Chamber offers networking opportunities, business development programs, and advocacy at various levels, while also assisting members with certifications, marketing, and growth initiatives.
