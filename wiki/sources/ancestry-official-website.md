- Ancestry sells family-history memberships, AncestryDNA kits, and bundled offerings that combine record access with DNA features.
- Product pages describe tree building, historical record search, genetic matches, origin estimates, traits features, family-history education, and AncestryPreserve digitization services.
- The company site lists related brands and services including Find a Grave, Fold3, Newspapers.com, AncestryProGenealogists, and AncestryPreserve.
- Ancestry's corporate team page lists Howard Hochhauser as president and chief executive officer. The prior malformed stub's Deb Liu CEO claim is therefore stale.
- The company facts page is the preferred official source for current scale claims, but exact numbers should still be treated as company self-reporting unless independently verified.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official company source. It is reliable for current product categories, headquarters, official leadership, and public positioning. It is weaker for market-size, database-size, and performance claims because those are self-reported and may change frequently. Use independent reporting, privacy-policy documents, and regulatory or legal records before treating sensitive claims about genetic-data access or law-enforcement use as high confidence.
