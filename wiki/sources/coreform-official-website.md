## Summary

Coreform's official website describes isogeometric analysis (IGA) software for running finite element analysis directly on fully featured CAD geometry, plus related meshing and preprocessing tools.

## Useful Claims

- The site positions Coreform around isogeometric analysis (IGA) software that lets engineers run FEA on fully featured CAD designs without defeatured geometry.
- Product families mentioned include Coreform IGA for Abaqus and Coreform Cubit for CAD geometry preprocessing and meshing.
- The site says Coreform supports linear and nonlinear, static and dynamic, structural and multiphysics analyses within a unified workflow.
- Use cases highlighted on the site span nuclear energy, automotive development, and other engineering domains.
- The site publishes customer testimonials from engineering analysts describing faster simulation workflows and reduced mesh-quality burden.
- The site offers contact sales and free-trial paths for commercial evaluation.
