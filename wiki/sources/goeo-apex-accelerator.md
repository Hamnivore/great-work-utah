## Summary

The GOEO APEX Accelerator page describes Utah's procurement-assistance program for businesses pursuing government contracts. It emphasizes government contracting readiness, defense industrial base access, training, workshops, and statewide one-on-one counseling.

## Useful Claims

- Utah APEX Accelerator helps Utah businesses find, bid on, and win procurement opportunities with the Department of Defense and federal, state, and local entities.
- The program offers free in-person one-on-one counseling through offices around the state.
- Support includes strategy, marketing tools, eligibility to become a government contractor, registration in government databases, small-business certification support, and solicitation document review.
- The page says the program is funded in part through a cooperative agreement with the Department of Defense.
