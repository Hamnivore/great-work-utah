## Summary

The GOEO Utah Center for Global Talent page describes a state center that helps businesses navigate pathways to recruit and retain global talent and supports economic opportunity for new Americans and foreign-born individuals following legal immigration pathways.

## Useful Claims

- The center was created by the Utah Legislature in 2021.
- It helps businesses navigate pathways to recruit and retain global talent.
- Its work includes coordination, strategy, convening, and advising around new Americans, foreign labor challenges, and workforce opportunities.
- The page names a partnership with The Center for Economic Opportunity and Belonging during the 2025-2026 state fiscal year.
- The page includes employer-facing resources on immigrant workforce integration, employment-based visa pathways, work authorization, naturalization, and related topics.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official program page. It is appropriate evidence for program purpose and broad service areas, but it is not a substitute for legal advice. Immigration-specific recommendations should route founders to qualified counsel or official resources.
