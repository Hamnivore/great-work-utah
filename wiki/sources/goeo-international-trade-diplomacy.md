## Summary

The GOEO International Trade & Diplomacy page describes Utah's international business promotion and trade-diplomacy work. It highlights export activity, diplomatic visits, trade missions, and the state's partnership with World Trade Center Utah.

## Useful Claims

- The page says Utah companies exported $17.4 billion in goods in 2023.
- It says 85% of Utah's 3,500-plus exporters are small- and medium-sized businesses.
- It says international trade supports more than 430,000 jobs in Utah.
- The page says GOEO hosts more than 40 diplomatic visits annually and partners with World Trade Center Utah for the governor's trade missions.
- It directs companies looking to expand into new markets to World Trade Center Utah for trade missions, trade shows, workshops, and grant opportunities.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official program page. Export statistics and diplomatic-program details should be refreshed periodically. For a specific company recommendation, verify the current World Trade Center Utah program or grant before suggesting an application.
