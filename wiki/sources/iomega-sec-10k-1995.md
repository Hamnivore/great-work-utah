## Summary

Iomega Corporation's annual 10-K report filed with the SEC in March 1995, covering the period in which the Zip drive was introduced. This is the primary corporate filing describing the Zip product, its March 1995 shipping date, and the parallel-port and SCSI versions available at launch.

## Useful Claims

- The Zip drive shipped in March 1995 as Iomega's new removable-media product.
- The original Zip drive was available in parallel-port (for IBM PC-compatibles) and SCSI (for Macs and PCs) versions.
- Iomega was a Utah-based corporation with Roy, UT as its principal operating location.
- The filing documents the company's storage-device product lines, manufacturing, and competitive positioning at the time of Zip's launch.
