## Summary

Jump Aero's official website describes the company's JA1 Pulse single-seat eVTOL aircraft designed for emergency medical services first response, and the company's mission to improve time-to-care in remote and terrain-constrained areas.

## Useful Claims

- The site presents the JA1 Pulse as a single-seat eVTOL purpose-built for ALS-level emergency first response, intended to reach patients ahead of ground ambulances.
- The company was founded by Carl Dietrich, previously co-founder of Terrafugia, a VTOL aircraft company.
- Jump Aero is headquartered in Petaluma, California.
- The JA1 Pulse is described as being in development as of 2025; the site does not confirm a production or certification date.
- Utah connection: Jump Aero participated in coverage planning with UDOT as part of the uFLY FAA eVTOL testbed program.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official company source for a development-stage product. Claims about vehicle performance, EMS operational integration, and certification timelines should be treated as aspirational rather than verified until independent flight test or regulatory documentation is available.
