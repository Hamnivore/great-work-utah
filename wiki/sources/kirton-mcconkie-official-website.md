## Summary

Kirton McConkie's official website describes the firm as a full-service Utah- and Idaho-based law firm with 200+ attorneys across five offices (Salt Lake City, Lehi, St. George, and Boise). Its practice pages cover corporate, intellectual property, real estate, tax, immigration, employment, and litigation work.

## Useful Claims

- The firm runs a corporate practice that advises on entity formation, capital raising and financing, securities compliance and private placements, and mergers and acquisitions. [Corporate practice page]
- The firm says clients frequently rely on it in a "general counsel" role, including scalable counsel for early-stage companies without full-time in-house legal staff.
- The Corporate/M&A group publicly names attorneys who advise emerging-growth companies and venture-capital financings (e.g., Robbie Yates, noted for an "emerging practice in the venture capital market" per Chambers USA).
- The firm reports recognition in Chambers USA, Best Lawyers, and the inaugural Legal 500 Salt Lake City Elite rankings.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official, self-published firm source. It is strong for describing the firm's stated capabilities, office footprint, and practice areas, and weaker as neutral proof of outcomes for any specific founder. Recognition claims (Chambers, Legal 500) are corroborated by those third-party rankings but should be treated as reputational signals, not guarantees of fit.
