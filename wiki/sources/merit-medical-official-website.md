## Summary

Merit Medical's official website describes the company's interventional, diagnostic, and therapeutic device portfolio across cardiology, radiology, endoscopy, and critical care. The site covers product lines, corporate history, investor relations (NASDAQ: MMSI), careers, and Merit OEM contract manufacturing services.

## Useful Claims

- Merit Medical describes itself as a manufacturer of disposable medical devices used in interventional, diagnostic, and therapeutic procedures — including catheters, micro-access kits, stents, ablation tools, and embolization devices.
- The site positions Merit's product portfolio across interventional cardiology, interventional radiology, endoscopy, and critical care.
- Corporate materials describe Merit as founded in 1987 by Fred Lampropoulos in South Jordan, Utah, with headquarters remaining in South Jordan.
- The company reports approximately $1.3 billion in annual revenue and more than 6,000 employees globally.
- Merit Medical trades on NASDAQ under the ticker MMSI.
- The Merit OEM page describes a contract manufacturing arm that extends Merit's manufacturing capabilities to third-party medical device clients.
- The site describes Martha Aronson as CEO as of late 2025, following the transition from founder Fred Lampropoulos.
