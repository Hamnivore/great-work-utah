## Summary

October 2025 press-release coverage announcing Merit Medical Systems' asset purchase of the C2 CryoBalloon gastrointestinal cryotherapy product line from Pentax Medical, with manufacturing transferring to South Jordan, Utah.

## Useful Claims

- Merit Medical (NASDAQ: MMSI) acquired Pentax Medical's C2 CryoBalloon technology in October 2025.
- Manufacturing of the C2 CryoBalloon line is being transferred into Merit's South Jordan facility.
- The transaction is consistent with Merit's broader pattern of acquiring adjacent interventional technologies and integrating them into its Utah footprint.
