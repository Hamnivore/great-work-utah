## Summary

NOW CFO's official site describes a financial consulting firm providing fractional CFO, controller, accounting, bookkeeping, financial operations, permanent-hire, and related finance-support services. The site presents a national footprint and uses an 801 phone number; the footer states that NOW CFO is not a CPA firm.

## Useful Claims

- The home page describes "fractional CFO & accounting support" and "flexible accounting and finance solutions built to scale."
- The services listed include budgeting and forecasting, financial planning and analysis, M&A, cash-flow management, KPIs and dashboards, board and investor reporting, treasury management, month-end close, audit preparation, financial reporting, transaction accounting, systems implementation, technical accounting, internal controls, chart-of-accounts design, AP, AR, payroll administration, and inventory management.
- The about page describes fractional, interim, project-based CFO services and full-time placement.
- The locations page and about page present a national service footprint.
- The footer states that NOW CFO is not a CPA firm.

## Reliability Notes
