## Summary

The Nucleus Grow page describes Nucleus Grow as Utah's official resource partner for SBIR/STTR support. It helps Utah companies with non-dilutive funding strategy, topic matching, proposal development, proposal review, agency-specific guidance, registrations, budgeting, workshops, and consultations.

## Useful Claims

- Nucleus Grow focuses on early-stage Utah companies in deep tech, life sciences, AI, advanced manufacturing, cleantech, and frontier innovation.
- Its services include topic matching, coaching, proposal review and editing, training, workshops, and one-on-one sessions.
- Consultations, editing, and guidance are available at no cost to Utah businesses, while some events may have nominal fees.
- Eligibility language says companies working with Nucleus Grow must be headquartered in Utah and registered with Utah's Division of Corporations, or be registered by SBIR/STTR submission.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official program page and is appropriate for public evidence about Nucleus Grow's services and eligibility. Funding amounts and workshop dates should be rechecked when used in a live recommendation.
