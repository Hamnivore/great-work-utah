## Summary

The Nucleus MarketEdge page describes a virtual, cohort-based program for professors, PhDs, and postdocs in Utah higher education who are exploring commercialization and startup formation.

## Useful Claims

- The program is designed for Utah university or technical-college professors, PhDs, and postdocs.
- Participants work with peers, founders, industry experts, and investors while learning about funding, market readiness, and startup formation.
- The page says the program is free and asks participants to commit 8-10 hours per week for 8 weeks.
- As accessed on 2026-05-09, the page said Fall 2026 registration was open until August 31 and that the cohort would run from September 28 to November 16.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official program page and is good evidence for audience, format, and stated application timing as of the access date. Application windows are time-sensitive and should be verified before recommending the program to a live researcher.
