## Summary

Nusano's official website describes a physics company building accelerator-based radioisotope production for medical, energy, and critical-minerals applications. Its flagship production facility is in West Valley City, Utah.

## Useful Claims

- Nusano supplies medical radioisotopes to drugmakers, hospitals, and clinics, targeting undersupplied markets for radiotherapeutics and diagnostics.
- The company combines patented particle-acceleration technology with established production methods to make radioisotopes at commercial scale.
- West Valley City, Utah is named as the site of a state-of-the-art production facility bringing high-tech jobs to the Salt Lake Valley.
- Beyond life sciences, the site describes applications in clean energy (accelerating decay of long-lived nuclear waste) and critical minerals recovery.
- Nusano frames its role as enabling infrastructure for next-generation cancer therapies dependent on isotopes such as those used in targeted radiopharmaceuticals.
