## Summary

Owlet's official website describes connected nursery products for infant monitoring, including FDA-cleared sock monitors, video monitors, bundled systems, and a prescription pulse-oximeter product for medical use at home.

## Useful Claims

- Owlet markets **Dream Sock** as an FDA-cleared best seller that monitors pulse rate, oxygen, and sleep in real time while the baby sleeps.
- **Dream Sight** is presented as a smart HD video baby monitor.
- **Dream Duo** is described as an integrated baby monitoring system combining sock and camera capabilities.
- **BabySat** is labeled FDA-cleared and Rx-only; the site describes it as a pulse oximeter that lets caregivers, under healthcare-provider guidance, monitor and customize pulse-rate and oxygen-saturation alarm limits for infants with medical needs at home.
- The site says Owlet tools have monitored billions of hours of baby sleep and promotes real-time alerts for vitals, cry, motion, and sound.
- Some products are advertised as eligible for HSA/FSA purchase; promotional pricing and sitewide discount codes appear on the homepage.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official company source. FDA clearance labels and wellness claims on a company site are leads, not independent verification of safety or clinical benefit. Regulatory status, intended use, and marketing boundaries for each SKU should be confirmed against FDA databases, product labeling, and third-party reporting — especially given Owlet's history of FDA scrutiny over prior Smart Sock marketing.
