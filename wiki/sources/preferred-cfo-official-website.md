## Summary

Preferred CFO's official website describes a Utah-based fractional/outsourced CFO firm founded in 2013, located in Salem, UT (greater Salt Lake City metro, ~20 minutes south of Provo). It provides fractional CFO leadership, outsourced controllers, financial systems advisory, and outsourced HR leadership.

## Useful Claims

- The firm provides fractional CFO services "to businesses that need experienced financial leadership without the commitment of a full-time hire," with engagements custom-tailored to business size, goals, and stage of growth. [Fractional CFO Services page]
- Listed CFO-level offerings include financial forecasting and reporting, cash-flow management, deal structuring and fundraising support, capital management, and guidance "preparing for fundraising or investors." [Services / Contact pages]
- The firm says it is based in Utah and that its fractional CFOs serve clients across multiple states; it offers a complimentary initial assessment before scoping an engagement. [Contact / Insights pages]
