## Summary

Rebel Medicine's official website describes the company as developing disruptive, non-opioid solutions for acute pain, with a lead asset Alevatrix™ positioned as a best-in-class long-acting local anesthetic for non-opioid postoperative pain management.

## Useful Claims

- The company states it is focused on developing disruptive, non-opioid solutions for acute pain.
- Rebel Medicine markets Alevatrix™ as a long-acting local anesthetic for non-opioid postoperative pain management.
- The site reports completion of a Pre-IND meeting for Alevatrix™ (September 30, 2024).
- The site reports publication of data from Alevatrix™ large-animal preclinical studies (August 6, 2024).
- The site reports NSF SBIR Phase 2b and NIH SBIR Phase 1 grant awards (October 11, 2023).
- Rebel Medicine is headquartered in Salt Lake City, Utah (per company press materials linked from the site).
