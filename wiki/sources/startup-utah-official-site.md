## Summary

Startup State is the official Utah Governor's Office of Economic Opportunity portal for Utah entrepreneurs. The site frames itself as a statewide entrepreneurship brand and business resource portal for people starting, growing, building, funding, connecting, and exiting businesses in Utah.

## Useful Claims

- The site is developed and maintained by GOEO's Startup State Initiative.
- The portal is meant to support Utah entrepreneurs across the business lifecycle.
- The site includes starting-business steps, growth resources, funding opportunities, events, news, and a resource filter.
- The site identifies itself as beta and asks users for feedback.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official state portal and is appropriate evidence for Startup State's stated purpose, navigation, and program positioning. Individual resource details should be verified on each resource provider's own official page before a founder receives a live next-step recommendation.
