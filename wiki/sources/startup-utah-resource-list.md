# Startup State Resource List

**Status:** Useful
**Confidence:** Medium

**Publisher:** Utah Governor's Office of Economic Opportunity

## Summary

The Startup State resource page presents a resource filter for Utah entrepreneurs. It invites users to search and filter resources by topic, stage, community, industry, and location, and includes routes to add a resource, download the resource list, print results, or email results.

## Useful Claims

- The page is positioned as "Resources for Your Business."
- The filter is intended to help entrepreneurs find resources specific to their business.
- The filter dimensions include topic, stage, community, industry, and location.
- The portal accepts resource submissions for review and alignment with its mission to support business growth across Utah.

## Reliability Notes

This page is official evidence that GOEO wants a resource-filter model and recognizes multiple matching dimensions. Because dynamic resource listings may change, specific resources should be corroborated with each provider's official page.
