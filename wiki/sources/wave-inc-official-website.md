## Summary

WAVE Charging's official website describes inductive wireless charging systems for commercial electric vehicle fleets. The company (legacy name WAVE, Wireless Advanced Vehicle Electrification) targets transit, port, airport, warehouse, and logistics fleets with automatic in-road charging at 125 kW to 500 kW+ power levels.

## Useful Claims

- WAVE delivers 125 kW to 500 kW+ wireless charging for commercial fleets, marketing automatic charging with no cables or plugs when vehicles park over ground pads.
- The site describes inductive power transfer through a 5- to 8-inch air gap at 92%+ efficiency using high-frequency electromagnetic fields.
- Solutions pages cover transit and shuttle opportunity charging, port and airport heavy equipment, and warehouse and distribution logistics workflows.
- Marketing claims include 30–50% fewer charging units versus plug-in infrastructure for some transit deployments, weather-independent operation, sealed systems with minimal maintenance, and no exposed live electrical components at the vehicle interface.
- The company offers site assessment, custom engineering design, installation support, and optional ongoing technical support services.
- Public business directories and the company's LinkedIn profile list headquarters at 4752 W California Ave, Salt Lake City, UT 84104.

## Reliability Notes

This is an official company source. Efficiency figures, infrastructure savings percentages, and deployment scale claims are company marketing — not independently audited.
