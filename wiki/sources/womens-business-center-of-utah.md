## Summary

The Women's Business Center of Utah offers advising, trainings, tools, templates, and community for Utah women entrepreneurs and business owners. Its advising page describes free one-on-one advising, startup and growth support, business planning, legal and foundational guidance, capital access, financial management, marketing, and operations support.

## Useful Claims

- WBCUtah offers free business advising, workshops, trainings, resources, and templates.
- Its advising page says new clients sign up for services before booking one-on-one advising.
- The organization lists Salt Lake City and Cedar City offices and describes statewide support.
- The site says WBCUtah is funded in part by the SBA, making complimentary services possible for Utah entrepreneurs.

## Reliability Notes

This is official evidence for WBCUtah's public service offering. Eligibility, appointment availability, and current programs should be checked on the official site before a live referral.
